Lipinski search

You can use an interactive parallel coordinates plot to filter your samples according to Lipinski's rule of five.

For this, simply select a range around the parameter of interest, e.g. logP, and the table will automatically update. You can also move the selection vertically or adjust the size of the range. By clicking on a vertical bar outside the selected range you can reset the selection.

Also, the table has a hoover functionality, which will highlight the line of a compound in the parallel coordinates plot.
